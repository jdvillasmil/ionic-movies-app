    - REVW-01: Submit review (1-10 range slider + text) — blocked on second attempt
    - REVW-02: Edit review — dual averages update immediately to new score
    - REVW-03: Delete review — dual averages recalculate to exclude deleted score
    - REVW-04: Guest can view reviews but cannot see the submit form
  </what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
    Run the app locally: `ionic serve` or `npx ng serve`

    **DETL-01 — Full detail page:**
    1. Open the app and tap any card on Home or Search tab.
    2. Verify: detail page opens with real movie/series title (not "próximamente" placeholder).
    3. Verify: poster image loads (use TmdbImagePipe w500 — full quality poster visible).
    4. Verify: synopsis (overview text) is displayed.
    5. Verify: release date is shown.
    6. Verify: genre chips/labels are displayed (e.g., "Acción", "Drama").
    7. Verify: cast section shows at least 3-5 actor names and character names.
    8. Verify: back button returns to previous page.

    **DETL-02 — Dual rating averages:**
    1. On any detail page with existing reviews (or after submitting one below).
    2. Verify: "Usuarios" section shows a numeric average (e.g., "7.5") and "(N reseñas)".
    3. Verify: "Críticos" section shows a separate numeric average and count.
    4. Verify: If no reviews yet, both show "0" with "(0 reseñas)".

    **DETL-03 — Review list:**
    1. On a detail page with at least one review submitted.
    2. Verify: each review card shows the author's display name.
    3. Verify: role badge visible (e.g., "Normal" or "Crítico").
    4. Verify: score badge shows "X/10".
    5. Verify: review text is displayed below.

    **REVW-04 — Guest access (no form):**
    1. Open the app without logging in (clear session if needed: DevTools → Application → Clear Storage).
    2. Navigate to any detail page.
    3. Verify: reviews list and dual averages ARE visible to the guest.
    4. Verify: the review submission form is NOT visible — should see "Inicia sesión para escribir una reseña" or similar prompt instead.

    **REVW-01 — Submit review:**
    1. Log in with a normal user account.
    2. Navigate to a detail page you have NOT reviewed yet.
    3. Verify: review form is visible with a range slider (score) and textarea (text).
    4. Set score to 8 using the slider, type a short review text.
    5. Tap the submit button.
    6. Verify: success toast appears ("Reseña enviada" or similar).
    7. Verify: the form disappears and is replaced by edit/delete controls.
    8. Verify: the Usuarios average updates to include your new score.
    9. Verify: your review appears in the reviews list with your name and role badge.

    **REVW-01 — Duplicate blocked:**
    1. While still on the same detail page after submitting:
    2. Verify: the submit form is gone — only edit/delete controls are visible.
    3. Alternatively: navigate away and back — verify the form still does not reappear (your existing review is detected).

    **REVW-02 — Edit review:**
    1. On a detail page where you already submitted a review.
    2. Note the current Usuarios average value.
    3. Change the score slider to a different value (e.g., from 8 to 4).
    4. Tap the edit/save button.
    5. Verify: the Usuarios average updates immediately to reflect the new score.
    6. Verify: your review in the list shows the new score.

    **REVW-03 — Delete review:**
    1. On a detail page where you have a review.
    2. Note the current Usuarios average and review count.
    3. Tap the delete button.
    4. Confirm deletion in the alert dialog.
    5. Verify: your review disappears from the reviews list.
    6. Verify: Usuarios average recalculates (if you were the only reviewer: shows 0 with 0 reseñas).
    7. Verify: the submit form reappears (you can now submit a new review).

    **Critic role (if available):**
    1. Log in with a critic account (role='critic').
    2. Submit a review on a title.
    3. Verify: the Críticos average updates — not the Usuarios average.
    4. Verify: role badge on your review shows "Crítico".

    **Profile page reviews list:**
    1. After submitting a review, navigate to your Profile tab.
    2. Verify: "Mis Reseñas" section shows the review you just submitted (title + score + text).
